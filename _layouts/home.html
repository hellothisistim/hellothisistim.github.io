About

Tim loves images. He has worked on them around the world, for feature films and television shows such as The Great Gatsby, True Detective, The Wolverine, Gravity, The Fault in Our Stars, and Boardwalk Empire.

His specialty is making highly complex tasks manageable in order to meet both deadlines and expectations.
