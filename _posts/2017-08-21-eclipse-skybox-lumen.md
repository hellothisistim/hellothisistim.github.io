Eclipse, 21 August 2017.
Scan of unfixed lumen print. 

This is an all-day lumen exposure from the skybox pinhole camera on 21 August 2017, the day of the eclipse in North America. The thinness in the line of the sun is when the moon slowly covered the sun, and then revealed it again. #lumenprint #pinholephotography #silvergelatin
